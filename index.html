About

Passionate software developer with a diverse background in teaching and customer service, now dedicated to building innovative applications and creating exceptional user experiences. I recently completed a rigorous coding bootcamp through App Academy, equipping me with a strong foundation in software development.

With my experience teaching ESL classes in South Korea and my role as a table games supervisor at a casino, I have developed exceptional communication skills and the ability to adapt to diverse environments. My friends and family often describe me as level-headed, tenacious, and personable, qualities that contribute to my collaborative approach in working with teams and delivering high-quality projects.

What drives me in coding is the opportunity to explore new ways to implement ideas. As I build an app, I always prioritize the user experience, striving to create intuitive interfaces and seamless interactions. I believe that understanding the user's perspective is essential to crafting successful software solutions.

The world of software engineering fascinates me because of its limitless possibilities. It allows me to combine my creativity and problem-solving skills to bring ideas to life. Additionally, I have a strong interest in art, music, and the power of expression. These diverse interests provide me with a unique perspective and inspire me to create engaging and visually appealing applications.

I am actively seeking opportunities to contribute my skills and dedication to a dynamic software development team. I am driven by friendly competition and a constant desire to improve, both personally and professionally. If you're looking for a motivated and collaborative software developer with a passion for creating impactful solutions, let's connect.
